What is Tarot ?
The Tarot is a deck of 78 cards, each with its own imagery,
symbolism and story.The 22 Major cards represent life's
karmic, and the 56 Minor cards reflect the trials.
History
Every year on Halloween, Wyrmwood launches a project in
honor of the spooky season. In past campaigns we have
featured some incredible work from artists like Crystal Sully
and Frank Frazetta. However, this campaign brings that to a
whole new level. We have brought together over seventy
artists to create a delightfully corrupted deck of Tarot Cards.
